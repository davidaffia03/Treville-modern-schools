About Treville

One school, three stages of growth.

STAGE 01

Nursery

Early learners build language, numeracy and social skills through guided play and gentle structure, preparing them for the classroom years ahead.

STAGE 02

Primary

Pupils strengthen core subjects — literacy, numeracy, basic science and civic values — within a structured timetable and small class sizes.

STAGE 03

Secondary

Students prepare for WAEC and NECO through focused subject specialisation, mentorship and a strong culture of academic discipline.

Admissions

How to enrol your child.

01

Pick up a form

Visit the school office in Nung Uyo Idoro or send an enquiry through the contact form below to request an admission form.

02

Submit documents

Return the completed form along with the required documents listed alongside.

03

Assessment

Primary and secondary applicants sit a short placement assessment to confirm the right class level.

04

Confirmation

Families receive confirmation and a resumption date, along with the term's fee structure.

What you'll need

  • Completed admission form
  • Birth certificate or age declaration
  • Two recent passport photographs
  • Previous school report (Primary & Secondary)
  • Immunisation record (Nursery)
