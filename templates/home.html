About Treville

One School. Three Stages. Endless Possibilities.

From a child's first classroom experience to senior secondary graduation, Treville Modern Schools provides a continuous journey of academic excellence, discipline and character development.

STAGE 01

Nursery School

A nurturing environment where curiosity, confidence and creativity are developed through guided learning and play.

STAGE 02

Primary School

Building strong foundations in literacy, numeracy, science, technology and moral values through engaging classroom experiences.

STAGE 03

Secondary School

Preparing future leaders for WAEC, NECO and higher education through quality teaching, innovation and discipline.

Academic Excellence Our Watchword

Every learner is inspired to grow intellectually, morally and socially in an environment that promotes confidence, leadership and lifelong learning.

Admissions

Join The Treville Family

Admission into Nursery, Primary and Secondary classes is open throughout the academic session subject to available spaces.

01

Obtain Admission Form

Visit the school or contact us online to obtain an admission form for your child.

02

Submit Required Documents

Attach birth certificate, passport photographs, previous results where applicable and every required document.

03

Placement Assessment

Qualified applicants sit a simple placement test before class allocation.

04

Congratulations

Successful applicants receive admission confirmation, fee schedule and resumption details.

Admission Checklist

  • Completed Admission Form
  • Birth Certificate
  • Four Passport Photographs
  • Last School Result
  • Medical Record (Nursery)
